Life Outside the Classroom

At St John’s High School – Kazo, learning extends beyond textbooks. Our co-curricular activities develop teamwork, confidence, creativity, discipline, and leadership — shaping well-rounded learners.

Football

Our Football team builds teamwork, resilience, and competitive spirit. Students participate in inter-school competitions, fostering discipline and school pride.

Netball

The netball team empowers students through teamwork and strategic play, encouraging physical fitness, confidence, and leadership among learners.

Chess

Chess sharpens critical thinking, patience, and decision-making skills. Learners develop focus and strategic planning through friendly competition.

Music, Dance & Drama (MDD)

MDD nurtures creativity, cultural appreciation, and self-expression, allowing students to showcase talent through performances and festivals.
