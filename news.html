A New Term Begins

The gates swing wide with morning light,
Shoes echo hope on dusty ground,
Young minds awake to dreams once more,
Where books and courage both abound.

Chalk meets board, ideas take flight,
Questions bloom in eager eyes,
Each lesson plants a silent seed,
For futures yet to touch the skies.

In halls of learning, hearts are shaped,
By discipline, by truth, by care,
Today we walk the path of growth,
Tomorrow’s leaders rising there.

By Katushabe Arthur - S.6 Arts
